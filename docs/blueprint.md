# **App Name**: TransGlobal Solutions

## Core Features:

- Service Catalog: Showcase 3 distinct logistic services with detailed technical specifications and real-world images.
- International Trade Data Integration: Include international trade and logistic related technical details to demonstrate the solution such as 'Incoterms', 'HS codes', or typical 'transit times'.
- Contact Form Submission: A working contact form, including address, email, and phone number.
- Multilingual Support: Allow content translation between English, French, and Spanish
- Legal Compliance Footer: Automatically generate the footer with links to 'Legal Notice', 'Privacy Policy' and 'Cookies Policy' pages.
- Image Enhancement: Use an AI tool to automatically improve uploaded logistics-related images, such as trucks and warehouses.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep orange (#FF8C00) to evoke energy and reliability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) providing a clean and professional feel.
- Accent color: Soft yellow (#F0E68C) for CTAs and visual highlights.
- Body text: 'PT Sans', sans-serif.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif.
- Use clear, modern icons representing logistical services, transport methods, and international trade.
- Maintain a grid layout to maintain consistency. A fully responsive design adapts for viewing on different screen sizes.
